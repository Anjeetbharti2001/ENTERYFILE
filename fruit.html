  1. What is Mango?

    2. Mango:- A mango is a tropical fruit with a
    fleshy edible pulp and a large seed inside.
    It is known as the "King of Fruits" in many countries.

  2. What is Apple?

    3. Apple: An apple is a sweet
    or slightly sour fruit with a thin skin, crisp flesh, and seeds inside.
    It is one of the most widely grown and consumed fruits in the world.

  3. What is Orange?

    4. Orange: An orange is a round, citrus fruit with a thick orange-colored peel and juicy,
    sweet or slightly sour flesh inside. or
    An orange is a nutritious fruit rich in vitamin C that grows on the orange tree,
    scientifically known as Citrus × sinensis.
